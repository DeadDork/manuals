Standard Functions (sin, cos etc.)

The names of certain standard functions and abbreviations are obtained by typing a backlash \ before the name. For example, one obtains

by typing 
\[ \cos(\theta + \phi) = \cos \theta \cos \phi
      - \sin \theta \sin \phi \]
